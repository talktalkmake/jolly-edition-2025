Frequently Asked Questions

How long does your process take?
3 weeks spread over as many months as you need. Typically that’s 12-18 months, depending on when you book your project.
Do you offer packages?
Laura offers a single package that can be customized to your individual needs upon request. (If you need a cocktail card but not a menu, no problem.) Clients interested in less than a full package will not be a good fit.
Is printing included?
Because Laura offers only a single package, she can offer the finest printing possible so that your project is just right, from beginning to end. All paper supply, printing, finishing, embellishments, assembly, packaging, and shipping to you is included for up-to 300 guests.
How much do you charge for printing?
Printing is included.
How do we communicate during the project?
Mostly by email, but there will be online presentations of the concept and each subsequent design. Voice notes keep us in touch between design weeks so, should anything change, we can keep informed.
How many projects are you working on?
To preserve the highest possible standards, Jolly Edition accepts only a handful of projects each year. The right clients and the right projects are the most important consideration when producing custom artwork. Anything less than a good fit isn’t going to make for happy clients.
When do I need to book my project?
Jolly Edition produces only a handful of projects each year. Inquire as soon as you read this to give yourself the best chance of working with her.
Do you work with same-sex couples?
You shouldn’t need to ask that, but to be clear: yes. Always have, always will ❤️
